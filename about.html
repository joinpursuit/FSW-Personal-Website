About Me

Hello my name is Joshua Mendez. I currently live in Hawthorne, NJ. I was raised in Clifton, NJ. I attended Bergen Community College for three years. It was there I took many different classes, but ultimately decided on Computer Science. Unfortunately I was not able to obtain a decree in it so I changed my major to general liberal arts in order to graduate sooner. During my time in Bergen Community College I also took part in some extra curricular activities such as Student Government as well as, Student life. I was in Student Government for 2 years. At my third year I was elected to become Secretary of SGA.
