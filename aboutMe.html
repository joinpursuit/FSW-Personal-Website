 About Me

Hi there!

As an innovator and creative, opportunities that combine design thinking and technology are where I thrive. The tension between the theoretical and the analytical feuled me to concurrently obtain two BS degrees from the Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT).

Turns out design and supply chain really do need one another to succeed.

Being a skilled Tech Designer, I am acutely aware of the implications of form, fit, and function. Software engineering fuses these concepts and provides additional avenues to meet the user's needs. From apparel to apps, and anything in-between - creating accessible and inclusive design is my core mission.

Away from the console, I enjoy traveling, dancing, and exploring the five boroughs on my bike.
