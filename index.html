ABOUT

Welcome! I am a software developer whose goal is to use my creativity and a multidisciplinary approach to improve accessibility at the intersection of art, entertainment, and technology. I am passionate about community building via information sharing and currently spend my days helping graduate students navigate the CUNY system, while further strengthening my coding skills at night through Pursuit, a selective, year-long, project-based Web Development Fellowship. I am curious about the layers of systems that govern our daily experiences. I enjoy learning about and discussing spirituality. I view working in tech as a mirror to better understanding the systems and language of our tangible world.
