For this Dice Roller project, I created key/value pairs of die face icons and their numerical value. The user's input dictates how many randomized dice would populate while simultaneously adding the face values. Displayed as the sum. Clicking 'Roll the dice!' sets this cascade in motion and as the user clicks the button again, the previous set of dice and accompanying sum populate the field below.
